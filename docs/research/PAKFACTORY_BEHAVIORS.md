# PakFactory Homepage Behaviors

- Header stays visually static while scrolling on desktop in the inspected session.
- Hero contains two CTA buttons: a green primary quote CTA and a white secondary packaging-style CTA.
- The line beneath the hero rotates the packaging category copy over time; during inspection it shifted between Apparel, Restaurant, Cosmetic, and Product Packaging.
- Product cards use simple hover elevation/color emphasis rather than complex motion.
- FAQ uses accordion-style disclosure with one question expanded by default.
- Mobile view collapses the layout into stacked sections and the product grid becomes fewer columns.

## Key Computed Values

- Body font: `Proxima Nova`, 16px, `rgb(53, 58, 63)`
- Heading font: `Sharp Sans`
- Primary green: `rgb(54, 179, 126)`
- Dark navy: `rgb(14, 23, 40)`
- Light neutral surface: `rgb(246, 246, 246)`
- Hero height: about `450px`
- Hero H1: `37px`, `700`, white
- Hero CTAs: `18px`, `600`, `16px 32px`, `6px` radius
