# PakFactory Homepage Component Spec

## Foundation

- Font stack:
  - Headings: `Sharp Sans`, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif
  - Body: `Proxima Nova`, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif
- Palette:
  - Green CTA: `rgb(54, 179, 126)`
  - Deep navy: `rgb(14, 23, 40)`
  - Body text: `rgb(53, 58, 63)`
  - Light gray section background: `rgb(246, 246, 246)`

## Hero

- Background image: `pakfactory-homepage-banner_4_.jpg`
- Height: about `450px`
- H1 width: about `522px`
- Primary CTA:
  - Label: `Request a Quote`
  - Background: green
  - Text: white
- Secondary CTA:
  - Label: `Choose Packaging style`
  - Background: white
  - Text: green

## Catalog Section

- Four-column desktop grid of product cards
- White cards with soft shadow and neutral image surface
- Card titles use heading font with dark navy
- Descriptions use smaller gray copy

## Services Section

- Split layout with explanatory copy and a central diagram image
- Process steps presented as pills
- Four supporting value cards underneath

## Footer

- Dark charcoal background
- Large collaborative headline
- Newsletter form
- Three text-link columns
- Sustainability and compliance support panels
