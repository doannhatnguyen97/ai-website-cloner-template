# PakFactory Homepage Topology

1. Announcement bar for tariff messaging.
2. Multi-row header with utility links, contact strip, logo, primary navigation, and quote CTA.
3. Hero banner with packaging photography background, headline, supporting copy, dual CTAs, and social proof.
4. Rotating "best solution for ..." strip.
5. Large product catalog grid for packaging categories.
6. Mid-page "Looking for something else?" custom quote callout.
7. Industry chip/grid section.
8. Sustainability split cards section.
9. Services / 360 PakFactory approach section with process chips and feature cards.
10. Manufacturer credibility section with image and supporting value props.
11. Reviews + FAQ zone.
12. Instagram gallery.
13. Repeated CTA banner.
14. Large dark footer with newsletter, link columns, sustainability, and compliance blocks.

## Notes

- The homepage is visually sectioned by alternating white and light-gray backgrounds.
- Cards consistently use soft shadows, thin borders, and `6px` corner radii.
- The strongest brand accents are green CTAs and dark blue/navy typography.
